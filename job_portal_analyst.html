Data Analyst Internship (Non-Paid)

Location: San Francisco, Remote

Apply for this position

Overview

Are you passionate about data and eager to learn in a startup environment? We're looking for a motivated Data Analyst intern to join us at ExamAi and work closely with our CEO on exciting projects.

This internship is a great opportunity to get hands-on experience with data analysis, web scraping, and database creation, while contributing to a fast-growing company. Even though this is a non-paid position, you'll gain invaluable experience and build skills that will make you stand out in your future career.

What You’ll Do

  • Analyze data: Help us understand patterns and insights from the data we collect.
  • Create databases: Organize and structure data that supports our decision-making and strategy.
  • Web scraping: Assist in gathering useful information from the web to keep us ahead in the game.
  • Work closely with the CEO: You'll have the chance to collaborate directly with our CEO on high-impact tasks that shape the future of ExamAi.

What We’re Looking For

  • Curiosity and eagerness to learn: You don’t need to be an expert, but you should be excited about diving into data and learning new tools.
  • Basic knowledge of data analysis tools: If you’re familiar with tools like Excel, Python, or any data-related software, that’s a plus!
  • Problem-solving mindset: You enjoy figuring things out and coming up with solutions.
  • Self-motivated and independent: You'll have guidance, but we're looking for someone who can also take initiative.

What You’ll Gain

  • Real-world experience: Work on meaningful projects that make a difference in our business.
  • Skill development: Learn and improve your data analysis, database creation, and web scraping abilities.
  • Mentorship: Receive direct guidance from our CEO and contribute to a fast-growing tech company.
